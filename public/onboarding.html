Welcome to Tree Style Tab

Your tabs, organized as a tree

Tree Style Tab organizes your browser tabs into a visual tree based on how you opened them. Tabs opened from another tab become its children, making it easy to see the relationship between your tabs.

Two Ways to Use

Choose the mode that fits your workflow

Tree Style Tab supports two modes — use the one you prefer, or switch between them anytime.

💬 Popup Mode
Alt+Q
  • Opens as an overlay on demand
  • Quick glance & tab switching
  • Closes automatically after action
NEW
📌 Side Panel Mode
Alt+S
  • Always visible alongside your pages
  • Real-time tab tree updates
  • Perfect for managing many tabs

💡 Try Alt + S after setup to open the side panel — it stays open while you browse!

🧭

Navigate Like a Pro

Full keyboard control for power users

↑ ↓ Move between tabs
← Collapse children / Go to parent
→ Expand children / Go to first child
Enter Switch to selected tab
Alt+W Close all sub-tabs

💡 You can also drag & drop tabs to reorganize the tree with your mouse.
